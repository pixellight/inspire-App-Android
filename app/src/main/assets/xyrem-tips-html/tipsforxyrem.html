Now that you have prepared your doses, always follow these safety tips when taking XYREM:

TIP 1:
Always take XYREM exactly as your doctor tells you to take it. Never change your XYREM dose without talking to your doctor.
TIP 2:
Read the Medication Guide before taking XYREM for the first time, and each time you get a refill. There may be new information.
TIP 3:
Do not take XYREM if you take other sleep medicines or sedatives (medicines that cause sleepiness), drink alcohol, or have a rare problem called succinic semialdehyde dehydrogenase deficiency.
TIP 4:
Do not drive a car, use heavy machinery, fly an airplane, or do anything that is dangerous or that requires you to be fully awake for at least 6 hours after you take XYREM. You should not do those activities until you know how XYREM affects you.
TIP 5:
Wait at least 2 hours after eating before you take XYREM.
Turn on the “Eat by” reminder function in the Plan Setup menu to set a meal reminder notification.
TIP 6:
Set an alarm to make sure you wake up for your second dose.
Use the “Time between doses” function in the “Plan” Setup menu to set an alarm to wake you up for your second dose. Check your phone settings to make sure your sound is turned “on”.
TIP 7:
Take your first XYREM dose at bedtime while you are in bed. Remain in bed after taking your dose.
TIP 8:
Take your second XYREM dose 2½ to 4 hours after you take your first dose. Remain in bed after taking your second dose of XYREM.
Tap “Confirm Dose Taken” after taking your second dose to automatically record your dose and update your Progress Report. Check “Reports” from the “Track” menu to see your progress.
TIP 9:
If you miss your second XYREM dose, skip that dose and do not take XYREM again until the next night. Never take 2 XYREM doses at 1 time.
TIP 10:
If you take too much XYREM, call your doctor or go to the nearest hospital emergency room right away.
TIP 11:
If for some reason you do not use a XYREM dose within 24 hours of mixing it with water, throw it away.
TIP 12:
XYREM is a controlled substance (CIII) because it contains sodium oxybate that can be a target for people who abuse prescription medicines or street drugs. Never give your XYREM to anyone else because it may cause death or harm them. Selling or giving away this medicine is against the law.
