EDS Severity

Everyone with narcolepsy experiences EDS. This means you have an overwhelming urge to sleep without warning, anywhere, anytime. It is most likely to happen in situations that don't require active participation, such as when you are watching TV or reading a book. But it can also happen when it is important to be alert, such as during conversations, eating, walking, or at work. You may nap several times a day, but only feel refreshed for a short time. This is one way that EDS is different from just feeling generally tired.


Key:


3 = High chance of dozing
2 = Moderate chance of dozing
1 = Slight chance of dozing
0 = Would never doze


Total Score:


0-10 = Normal
11-12 = Mild
13-15 = Moderate
16-24 = Severe
